Frequently Asked Questions

E-Waste management regulations and disposal methods.

  • What is e-waste management?

    E-waste management, also known as electronic waste management or e-waste recycling, refers to the process of collecting, disposing of, and recycling electronic devices and equipment at the end of their lifecycle.

  • What are the different types of e-waste?

    E-waste includes a diverse array of devices and components,such as:-

    Computers and Accessories: Desktop computers Laptop computers Monitors (CRT and LCD) Keyboards, mice, and other peripherals Printers and scanners

    Consumer Electronics: Televisions (CRT, LED, LCD, plasma) Audio and video equipment (DVD players, VCRs, speakers, amplifiers) Cameras and camcorders Game consoles

    Mobile Devices: Smartphones Tablets Mobile phones Portable media players
